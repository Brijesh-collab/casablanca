About Us

Casa Blanca - A boutique Resort is fusion of royal, heritage art of Ahmedabad and modern party culture of Spain.This resort is an absolute jewel amid one of the most alluring, pristine environments. Casablanca resort is where uncompromising freedom and rejuvenation meet luxurious accommodations, unparalleled service and five star facilities. Experience flamboyant moments that simply create awe, weave magic and leave you creating wonderful memories to cherish for long time.

Amenities

Garden

Earthy heaven to make you feel calm and give you peace of mind and soul. It is a perfect blend of elements of texture, color, and seasonal blooms. Experience nature in its purest form.

Poolside

Our open-air, outdoor pool offers unrivaled vistas of lush greenery. It is designed to enjoy swimming in natural surroundings during day time and under star-filled skies at night.

Playzone

A specially designed all-glass sports arena to make your stay more fun-filled and adventurous.

Gazebo

Sit back and relax in the private victorian gazebo facing the pool.Cool polished floors and timber details, a subtle color palette and stylish decor create a uniquely relaxed villa ambiance.

Rooftop Tent

Generously proportioned and designed in a light and airy open-plan villa style.The rooftop dining provides a special touch and connection with nature. The mosaic seating adds to the beauty of it.

Terrazzo

Sit, chill and enjoy succulent food amid melodious music at cafe terrazzo. It is specially crafted with cozy seating arrangement which enables refreshment, fabulous views and relaxation

Bar
