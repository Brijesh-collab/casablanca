Story of Origin

Being a big-time traveler, my all-time favorite place has been in Spain. I feel that Spain, in a way, resonates with India with its cultural proximity and architectural forms. My vision was to mix a little bit of Spain,to bring out the perfect fusion possible. The name Casa Blanca, itself suggests White Villa. Casa Blanca's family ensures to provide that to its customers. It’s individually customized rooms with a heritage touch that makes your holidays more unique. It is a luxurious retreat and provides the culturally beautiful yet contemporary saga of today in its well-built boutique resort.

-Milind Shah(Managing Director)
